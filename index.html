Growing Global Issue

1.81 billion people globally are affected by musculoskeletal (MSK) conditions, accounting for 20% of the world's population.

Nearly a quarter of MSK problems stem from osteoarthritis, with 365 million people specifically suffering from Knee Osteoarthritis (KOA) in 2019 - affecting nearly 1 in 20 people worldwide.

LM Health

Founded in 2025 at the University of Bath, LM Health is a pioneering medical technology company developing modular healthcare solutions for pain relief and rehabilitation. Our innovative approach combines user-centered design with sustainable medical technology. Our flagship product the LM Health Modular Knee Brace brings the needs of pain relief and rehabilitation into one device, providing care to patients anytime and anywhere.

Modular Knee Brace
